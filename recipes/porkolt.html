Porkolt

  • 2 lbs. beef
  • 1 large white onion
  • 3 oz. fat or butter
  • 3 tbs. ground paprika
  • 4 cups stock or broth
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  1. heat fat over medium heat in large stew-pot
  2. slice onion in half and slice each half into thin half-rings, and saute in the melted fat.
  3. add salt and pepper
  4. while onion is cooking stir occasionally and cube the beef. a moderate amount of fat is desireable to don't trim
  5. once the onion has browned and is very soft add the cubed beef and enough stock to cover it
  6. add half of the paprika and black pepper to taste
  7. bring the pot to a simmer and cover
  8. our goal is extremely tender meat and a thick sauce so uncover and begin reducing the stock when the meat is abuot 2/3s done
  9. after reducing 90% of the liquid add the remaining paprika and salt and pepper to taste
  10. continue to cook uncovered until sauce is thick and meat is tender
