Jewish Rice

I don't know why this is called "jewish" rice, it is definitely not kosher, but it is very satisfying and very easy. I have tried many substitution and additions and they generally work but I would not recommend anything but basmati rice.

  • 8oz of fresh sliced mushrooms (canned are fine)
  • 3/4 stick (6tbs) of butter
  • two 10oz cans of Campbell's beef consume
  • one medium white onion
  • bazmati rice, enough to fill both 10oz soup cans.
  • water, enough to fill both cans twice
  1. saute the mushrooms in half of the butter until tender
  2. grease a large baking dish
  3. dice the onion
  4. empty both cans consumme into the baking dish
  5. using one of the empty cans measure two cans of rice and two cans of water into the baking dish
  6. add the mushrooms butter and onions
  7. place baking dish uncovered into an oven at 350 degrees
  8. bake without stirring for 45 minutes or until the top is nicely browned.
